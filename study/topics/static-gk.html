Q.No 38 Static GKEnglish
The religious group saw the highest decadal population growth rate in India between the 2001 and 2011 censuses.
A
Sikh
B
Muslim
✓ Correct
C
Buddhist
D
Hindu

Solution

Correct answer: B. Between the 2001 and 2011 censuses, the Muslim community recorded the highest decadal population growth rate among India's religious groups.

A is incorrect: The Sikh community had a lower growth rate.

C is incorrect: The Buddhist community grew more slowly.

D is incorrect: The Hindu community grew more slowly than the Muslim community.

💡 The overall national population growth rate has been declining decade on decade across communities.

✓ Correct
