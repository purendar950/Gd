Q.No 38 Static GKEnglish
The religious group saw the highest decadal population growth rate in India between the 2001 and 2011 censuses.
A
Sikh
B
Muslim
✓ Correct
C
Buddhist
D
Hindu

Solution

Correct answer: B. Between the 2001 and 2011 censuses, the Muslim community recorded the highest decadal population growth rate among India's religious groups.

A is incorrect: The Sikh community had a lower growth rate.

C is incorrect: The Buddhist community grew more slowly.

D is incorrect: The Hindu community grew more slowly than the Muslim community.

💡 The overall national population growth rate has been declining decade on decade across communities.
Q.No 37 Static GKHindi
जनगणना 20 के अनुसार, भारत में निम्नलिखित में से कौन सा धार्मिक समूह अधिकतर शहरी क्षेत्रों में केंद्रित है? ।. जैन ||. ईसाई
A
Only I
✓ Correct
B
Only II
C
Both I and II
D
Neither I nor II

Solution

Correct answer: A. According to Census 2011, the Jain community (I) is the most urbanised religious group in India, largely concentrated in urban areas; Christians (II) are not predominantly urban.

B is incorrect: 'Only II' is wrong; Christians are not the most urban-concentrated group.

C is incorrect: 'Both' is wrong because Christians are not predominantly urban.

D is incorrect: 'Neither' is wrong since Jains are highly urban.

💡 Jains have among the highest literacy rates of any community in India.
