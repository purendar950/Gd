Q.No 26 Books & AuthorsEnglish
Frankenstein is written by :
A
Mary Shelley
✓ Correct
B
Bram Stoker
C
H.G. Wells
D
Robert Louis Stevenson

Solution

Correct answer: A. Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus (1818) was written by the English author Mary Shelley.

B is incorrect: Bram Stoker wrote Dracula (1897).

C is incorrect: H.G. Wells wrote science-fiction classics such as The Time Machine and The War of the Worlds.

D is incorrect: Robert Louis Stevenson wrote Treasure Island and Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde.

💡 Mary Shelley wrote Frankenstein at the age of 18-19; it is often considered the first true science-fiction novel.

Q.No 25 Books & AuthorsEnglish
Which poetic collection by Sumitranandan Pant won him the Jnanpith Award?
A
Pallav
B
Kala Aur Budhdha Chand
C
Chidambara
✓ Correct
D
Veena

Solution

Correct answer: C. The poetic collection 'Chidambara' won Sumitranandan Pant the Jnanpith Award (for 1968), making him one of the early recipients of the honour.

A is incorrect: 'Pallav' is an earlier important collection by Pant but did not win him the Jnanpith.

B is incorrect: 'Kala Aur Budhdha Chand' is another of his works, not the Jnanpith-winning one.

D is incorrect: 'Veena' was one of his early collections, not the award-winning book.

💡 Sumitranandan Pant was a leading poet of the Chhayavaad (Romantic) movement in Hindi literature.
Q.No 27 Books & AuthorsHindi
स्वदेशी आंदोलन की आलोचना करने वाली पुस्तक "घरे-बाईरे" (घर और दुनिया) किस बंगाली साहित्यकार द्वारा लिखी गई थी?
A
बंकिम चंद्र चट्टोपाध्याय
B
शरत चंद्र चट्टोपाध्याय
C
रवीन्द्रनाथ टैगोर
✓ Correct
D
भबानी भट्टाचार्य

Solution

Correct answer: C. 'Ghare-Baire' (The Home and the World), which critically examines the Swadeshi movement, was written by Rabindranath Tagore.

A
R.K. Narayan
B
Raja Rao
✓ Correct
C
Mulk Raj Anand
D
Khushwant Singh

Solution

Correct answer: B. 'The Serpent and the Rope' was written by Raja Rao, one of the major figures of early Indian English literature.

In which year did the Jaipur Literature Festival begin?
A
2002
B
2005
C
2006
✓ Correct
D

Solution

Correct answer: C. The Jaipur Literature Festival began in 2006 and has grown into one of the world's largest free literary festivals.
