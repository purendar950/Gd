A
2020
B
2021
✓ Correct
C
2022

Solution

Correct answer: B. India assumed the BRICS Chairship in 2021, hosting the 13th BRICS Summit virtually that year.

Q.No 35 Current AffairsEnglish
Which micro-missile system did India test in 2025?
A
Astra-ll
B
Vibhavasu
C
Bhargavastra
✓ Correct
D
Pralay

Solution

Correct answer: C. Bhargavastra is a low-cost micro-missile counter-drone system that India tested in 2025, designed to neutralise swarms of small drones.

A is incorrect: Astra is a beyond-visual-range air-to-air missile, not the 2025 micro-missile counter-drone system.

B is incorrect: Vibhavasu is not the system tested for this purpose.

D is incorrect: Pralay is a short-range ballistic missile, not a micro counter-drone system.

💡 Counter-drone systems like Bhargavastra are increasingly important against the growing threat of low-cost drone swarms.
Q.No 38 Current AffairsEnglish
In , the Centre officially announced the scrapping of the 'No-Detention Policy' for Classes 5 and 8 in central government-run schools?
A
November 2024
B
December 2024
✓ Correct
C
January 2025
D
October 2024

Solution

Correct answer: B. In December 2024, the Centre officially scrapped the 'No-Detention Policy' for Classes 5 and 8 in central-government-run schools, allowing students who fail year-end exams to be held back (after a re-test).

A is incorrect: November 2024 is not when the official announcement was made.

C is incorrect: January 2025 is after the December 2024 announcement.

D is incorrect: October 2024 is incorrect.

💡 The No-Detention Policy had been part of the Right to Education Act; it was diluted by a 2019 amendment empowering governments to decide on detention.
