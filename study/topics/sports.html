Q.No 28 SportsEnglish
In which city and year were the first World Athletics Championships held?
A
Helsinki, 1983
✓ Correct
B
Tokyo, 1991
C
Rome, 1987
D
Berlin, 1993

Solution

Correct answer: A. The first World Athletics Championships were held in Helsinki, Finland, in 1983.

B is incorrect: Tokyo hosted the 1991 edition (and again in 2007).

C is incorrect: Rome hosted the 1987 World Championships.

D is incorrect: The 1993 Championships were held in Stuttgart, not Berlin (Berlin hosted in 2009).

💡 Helsinki's Olympic Stadium, which hosted the 1952 Olympics, was the venue for the inaugural 1983 Championships.

What is the length of a standard field hockey pitch?
A
80 m
B
91.4 m
✓ Correct
C
100 m
D
105m

Solution

Correct answer: B. A standard field-hockey pitch is 91.4 metres long (100 yards) and 55 metres wide.

A is incorrect: 80 m is shorter than the regulation length.

C is incorrect: 100 m is the length of a standard football pitch's playing area, not a hockey pitch.

D is incorrect: 105 m is the typical length of a full-size football (soccer) field.

💡 The dimensions come from the old imperial measure of 100 yards by 60 yards.

Q.No 29 SportsEnglish
विश्व तीरंदाजी को अंतर्राष्ट्रीय ओलंपिक समिति (00) द्वारा तीरंदाजी के लिए अंतर्राष्ट्रीय शासी निकाय के रूप में आधिकारिक तौर पर किस वर्ष मान्यता मिली?
A
1932
B
1972
✓ Correct
C
1980
D
1992

Solution

Correct answer: B. Archery returned to the Olympic programme in 1972, the year associated with the international governing body's formal recognition for the sport at the Olympics.

A is incorrect: 1932 is too early; archery was absent from the Olympics for decades after 1920.

C is incorrect: 1980 is after the 1972 reinstatement.

D is incorrect: 1992 is far later than the recognition year.

💡 World Archery (formerly FITA) was founded in 1931 and governs the sport internationally.
Q.No 32 SportsEnglish
क्रिकेट विश्व कप की मेजबानी भारत ने पहली बार किस वर्ष की थी?
A
1975
B
1983
C
1987
✓ Correct
D
1996

Solution

Correct answer: C. India first hosted (co-hosted) the Cricket World Cup in 1987 (the Reliance Cup), shared with Pakistan - the first World Cup held outside England.

A is incorrect: 1975 was the first-ever World Cup, held in England.

B is incorrect: 1983 was the year India won the World Cup, but it was played in England.

D is incorrect: 1996 was the next subcontinent World Cup (India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka), not the first time India hosted.

💡 India later co-hosted the 2011 World Cup and won it, and hosted the 2023 World Cup solo.
Q.No 33 SportsEnglish⚠ low OCR confidence
प्रथम आधिकारिक विश्व शतरंज चैंपियन किसे माना जाता है?
A
Bobby Fischer
B
Wilhelm Steinitz
✓ Correct
C
Garry Kasparov
D
Paul Morphy

Solution

Correct answer: B. Wilhelm Steinitz is regarded as the first official World Chess Champion, holding the title from 1886 to 1894.

A is incorrect: Bobby Fischer became World Champion in 1972, nearly a century later.

C is incorrect: Garry Kasparov was World Champion from 1985, in the modern era.

D is incorrect: Paul Morphy was a dominant unofficial player of the 1850s but was never the official world champion.

💡 Steinitz is also credited with developing the principles of positional (strategic) chess.

Q.No 30 SportsEnglish
When did India's men's national football team last qualify for the Olympic Games?
A
1956
B
1960
✓ Correct
C
1964
D
1972

Solution

Correct answer: B. India's men's national football team last qualified for the Olympic Games in 1960 (Rome Olympics).

A is incorrect: 1956 (Melbourne) was India's best Olympic football performance (4th place), but they qualified again afterwards.

C is incorrect: India did not qualify for the 1964 Olympics football event.

D is incorrect: 1972 is later than India's last Olympic football appearance.

💡 At the 1956 Melbourne Olympics, India finished fourth - the best-ever finish by an Asian football team at that time.
