Q.No 28 SportsEnglish
In which city and year were the first World Athletics Championships held?
A
Helsinki, 1983
✓ Correct
B
Tokyo, 1991
C
Rome, 1987
D
Berlin, 1993

Solution

Correct answer: A. The first World Athletics Championships were held in Helsinki, Finland, in 1983.

B is incorrect: Tokyo hosted the 1991 edition (and again in 2007).

C is incorrect: Rome hosted the 1987 World Championships.

D is incorrect: The 1993 Championships were held in Stuttgart, not Berlin (Berlin hosted in 2009).

💡 Helsinki's Olympic Stadium, which hosted the 1952 Olympics, was the venue for the inaugural 1983 Championships.
