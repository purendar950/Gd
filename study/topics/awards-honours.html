Q.No 40 Awards & HonoursHindi
भारत रत्न पुरस्कार प्राप्त करने वाली पहली महिला कौन थी?
A
अरुणा आसफ अली
B
लता दीनानाथ मंगेशकर
C
इंदिरा गांधी
✓ Correct
D
मदर मैरी टेरेसा

Solution

Correct answer: C. Indira Gandhi was the first woman to receive the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1971.

A is incorrect: Aruna Asaf Ali received the Bharat Ratna in 1997, much later.

B is incorrect: Lata Mangeshkar received the Bharat Ratna in 2001.

D is incorrect: Mother Teresa received the Nobel Peace Prize (1979) but not the Bharat Ratna.

💡 The Bharat Ratna was instituted in 1954; Indira Gandhi received it while serving as Prime Minister.

2025 में, भारत-डेनमार्क संबंधों को मजबूत करने के लिए नाइट क्रॉस ऑफ द ऑर्डर ऑफ डैनब्रोग से किसे सम्मानित किया गया?
A
विजय शंकर
✓ Correct
B
राजनाथ सिंह
C
आशिम कुमार घोष
D
नटराजन चंद्रशेखरन

Solution

Correct answer: A. Per the answer key, the person in option A was honoured with the Knight Cross of the Order of the Dannebrog in 2025 in connection with strengthening India-Denmark relations.

B is incorrect: Rajnath Singh, the Defence Minister, is not the recipient indicated here.

C is incorrect: Ashim Kumar Ghosh is not the awardee in this context.

D is incorrect: N. Chandrasekaran is not credited with this Danish honour.

💡 The Order of the Dannebrog is one of Denmark's oldest and most prestigious orders of chivalry.
