B

C

✓ Correct
D

Solution

Correct answer: C. Indira Gandhi was the first woman to receive the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1971.

💡 The Bharat Ratna was instituted in 1954; Indira Gandhi received it while serving as Prime Minister.
