Q.No 40 Awards & HonoursHindi
भारत रत्न पुरस्कार प्राप्त करने वाली पहली महिला कौन थी?
A
अरुणा आसफ अली
B
लता दीनानाथ मंगेशकर
C
इंदिरा गांधी
✓ Correct
D
मदर मैरी टेरेसा

Solution

Correct answer: C. Indira Gandhi was the first woman to receive the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1971.

A is incorrect: Aruna Asaf Ali received the Bharat Ratna in 1997, much later.

B is incorrect: Lata Mangeshkar received the Bharat Ratna in 2001.

D is incorrect: Mother Teresa received the Nobel Peace Prize (1979) but not the Bharat Ratna.

💡 The Bharat Ratna was instituted in 1954; Indira Gandhi received it while serving as Prime Minister.
