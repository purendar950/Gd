Q.No 21 Polity & ConstitutionEnglish
Which body recommends the distribution of tax revenues between union and states?
A
NITI Aayog
B
Parliament
C
Finance Commission
✓ Correct
D
Planning Commission

Solution

Correct answer: C. The Finance Commission (Article 280) recommends the distribution of the net proceeds of taxes between the Union and the States and the principles governing grants-in-aid to the States.

A is incorrect: NITI Aayog is an advisory think-tank with no role in tax devolution.

B is incorrect: Parliament legislates on taxes but does not recommend the sharing formula.

D is incorrect: The Planning Commission, now abolished, dealt with development planning, not tax devolution.

💡 The Finance Commission is constituted by the President every five years (or earlier if needed).
Q.No 22 Polity & ConstitutionEnglish
During a financial emergency under Article 360, the president can:
A
Reduce salaries of government employees
✓ Correct
B
Dismiss State Governments
C
Dissolve Parliament
D
Suspend Fundamental Rights

Solution

Correct answer: A. During a Financial Emergency under Article 360, the President can direct the reduction of salaries and allowances of government employees, including judges of the Supreme Court and High Courts, to maintain financial stability.

B is incorrect: Dismissing state governments is a feature of President's Rule under Article 356, not a Financial Emergency.

C is incorrect: Dissolving Parliament is not a power granted under Article 360.

D is incorrect: Suspension of Fundamental Rights is linked to a National Emergency under Article 352, not a Financial Emergency.

💡 A Financial Emergency under Article 360 has never been declared in India.

B
It introduces tabla and harmonium
C
It uses many raga names still in use today
✓ Correct
D
It rejects traditional Indian theories

Solution

Correct answer: C. The Raga Tarangini is considered historically important because it records and uses many raga names that are still in use in Hindustani music today, providing continuity with earlier tradition.

Q.No 24 Science & TechnologyEnglish
Archimedes’ principle states that a body immersed in a fluid experiences an upward force equal to
A
Its own weight
B
The volume of the body
C
The weight of the fluid displaced
✓ Correct
D
The density of the fluid

Solution

Correct answer: C. Archimedes' principle states that a body immersed (fully or partly) in a fluid experiences an upward buoyant force equal to the weight of the fluid it displaces.

A is incorrect: The buoyant force equals the displaced fluid's weight, not the body's own weight (these are equal only when the body floats).

B is incorrect: The volume of the body is a quantity, not a force.

D is incorrect: Density of the fluid alone is not a force; the upthrust depends on weight of fluid displaced.

💡 This principle explains why ships float and why objects feel lighter in water.

Central Himalayas

Eastern Himalayas

Solution

Q.No 32 Science & TechnologyEnglish⚠ low OCR confidence
if an astronaut’s mass is 70 kg, what will be their weight on the Moon (1/6th of Earth's gravity)?
A
700 N
B
117 N
✓ Correct
C
420 N
D
686 N

Solution

Correct answer: B. Weight on the Moon = mass x lunar gravity = 70 kg x (9.8/6) m/s^2 ≈ 70 x 1.63 ≈ 114-117 N.

A is incorrect: 700 N (using g = 10) would be the approximate weight on Earth, not the Moon.

C is incorrect: 420 N would imply gravity far higher than one-sixth of Earth's.

D is incorrect: 686 N (70 x 9.8) is the weight on Earth, not on the Moon.

💡 Mass stays constant everywhere; only weight (= mass x local gravity) changes between the Earth and the Moon.

Consumer Price Index
C
Index of Industrial Production

Wholesale Price Index
✓ Correct

Solution

inadequacy of fertile soils across major regions
B
High dependence on monsoonal variability
✓ Correct
C
Chronic shortage of agricultural labor
D
Weakness of domestic market linkages

Solution

Correct answer: B. High dependence on the monsoon (rainfall variability) is the most critical structural constraint on Indian agricultural productivity, since a large share of farmland is rain-fed and vulnerable to erratic rains.
