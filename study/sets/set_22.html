Q.No 21 Polity & ConstitutionEnglish
The 73rd constitutional anendment deals with:
A
Anti-defection
B
Panchayati Raj
✓ Correct
C
Tribunals
D
Cooperative Societies

Solution

Correct answer: B. The 73rd Constitutional Amendment (1992) deals with Panchayati Raj, giving constitutional status to rural local self-government.

A is incorrect: Anti-defection is in the 52nd Amendment (Tenth Schedule).

C is incorrect: Tribunals were added by the 42nd Amendment (Articles 323A/323B).

D is incorrect: Cooperative societies were addressed by the 97th Amendment.

💡 The 74th Amendment (1992) similarly gave constitutional status to urban local bodies (municipalities).

Q.No 36 Science & TechnologyEnglish
What is the primary function of a fire extinguisher?

To add more fuel to the fire
B
To increase the temperature of the fuel
C
To increase the supply of air
D
To cut off the supply of air, reduce the temperature of the fuel, or both
✓ Correct

Solution

Correct answer: D. A fire extinguisher works by cutting off the air (oxygen) supply, reducing the temperature of the fuel, or both - removing the conditions needed for combustion.

C is incorrect: It does not increase the air supply.
